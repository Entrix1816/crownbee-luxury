Our Journey

THE CROWNBEE STORY

From a ₦1,000 ice block business to a multi-city luxury real estate empire.

Every great empire begins with a single step. For Crownbee Global Services Ltd, that step was a ₦1,000 ice block business — a humble venture founded by Amb Dr Mrs Olubunmi Mary Gbadewole and her husband, driven by grit, faith, and an unwavering belief that small beginnings could lead to extraordinary destinations.

What started as a modest trade selling ice blocks gradually grew into a thriving enterprise. Through discipline, reinvestment, and a sharp eye for opportunity, the couple transitioned from ice blocks into real estate — a move that would forever change their lives and the lives of hundreds of families across Nigeria.

"The ice block taught us patience. It taught us that every block, no matter how small, contributes to building something monumental. We applied that same principle to real estate — one plot, one client, one legacy at a time."

The brand was officially registered in 2018 as Crownbee Global Services Ltd. By 2021, they had developed their first major project: the Crownbee Luxury Apartment in Lugbe, Abuja. This was followed by Crownbee Prime Estate Abuja in 2022, and the prestigious Amaa Gee Luxe Court in Bodija, Ibadan across 2023 2024.

Today, Crownbee Global stands as a testament to what is possible when vision meets perseverance. With a portfolio spanning West Africa, East Africa, and the United States, the company continues to expand its footprint, guided by the same principles that transformed ₦1,000 into a luxury real estate dynasty.
