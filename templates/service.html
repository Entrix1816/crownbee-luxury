Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What type of land titles do you provide? +
We provide properties with Certificate of Occupancy (C of O), deed of assignment, and verified survey plans. All our properties have 100% clean titles with zero disputes.
Can I buy property from abroad? +
Yes! We have a dedicated Diaspora service that includes virtual tours, remote documentation, e-signatures, and secure online payment processing.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans on most properties. Typically 3-12 months installment options are available depending on the property.
What is the expected return on investment? +
Properties in our estates typically appreciate 30-50% over 2-3 years. Off-plan investments can yield even higher returns (40-60%).
Do you handle legal documentation? +
Absolutely. Our legal team handles all documentation including title verification, deed preparation, and final handover. Legal support is included in your purchase.
Can I resell my property through Crownbee? +
Yes, we offer resale support for our existing clients. Many investors have successfully resold their properties through our network.
