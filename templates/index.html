Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What type of land titles do you offer? +

We deal exclusively in government-approved titles, primarily Certificates of Occupancy (C of O) and Deeds of Assignment, ensuring your investment is 100% secure and legally protected. Every property undergoes rigorous due diligence before being listed.

Can I pay in installments? +

Absolutely. We offer flexible, structured payment plans tailored to your budget. Speak with our team to find a plan that works best for you. Many of our estates feature zero-interest installment options spread over comfortable periods.

What is an off-plan investment? +

Buying off-plan means securing your property at today's price before construction is completed. This allows you to benefit from significant capital appreciation — typically 25-40% — by the time the project is finished. It's one of the smartest ways to build wealth through real estate.

Which locations do you cover? +

We operate across Nigeria with a strong presence in Abuja (Headquarters), Lagos, Ibadan, and Osun State. We also facilitate property acquisitions nationwide through our extensive network of verified partners and agents.

How quickly can I secure a property? +

Once you've selected your plot or home, the documentation and transfer process typically takes 48 hours for immediate-payment purchases. Our legal team ensures a smooth, transparent handover with complete peace of mind.
